Final Images Justification

The images used in my page want to show a first-bite of War and Peace. Due to the great book, it is too large to introduce it briefly. Therefore, I choose more common and easy-to-catch figures.

In the cover page, the picture of dance invitation is aimed to form first interests to the book. It is peaceful, and the colour is illustrated nicely.

In the about page, Leo's figure is put at first to make the impression of one of the great authors. Then, it shows two figures about wars happened and written in the book. Compared to the peaceful in cover page, this book truly narrates horrible wars.

The background image in cover page which I processed with PhotoShop to make ten different book covers into one image and using mask to change the opacity.
